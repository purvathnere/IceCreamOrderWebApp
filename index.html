our speciality

Chocolate

Chocolate is a crowd favorite because, like vanilla, it's versatile. Unlike vanilla, it has a lot of variations: chocolate chunk, fudge swirl, mudslide. Along with that, chocolate ice cream is a favorite of many because it can be rich or light. It can also be dressed up in a sundae or eaten by itself.

Cookies & Cream

Cookies & cream is a new take on vanilla, because it has chunks of cookies so your customers can eat a lot without getting bored with their ice cream. This is also great ice cream for waffle cones and ice cream sandwiches. Try making your own with this waffle cone mix and waffle cone maker!

cold ice-cream

Finally, this is classic ice cream with vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry flavors. It's the perfect mix of three sweet things. This frozen treat would be fun to use in a banana split because every scoop could have all three flavors! Try using banana split containers to elevate your favorite treat.

Mint Chocolate Chip

This minty ice cream is a favorite because it's unlike other ice creams. It's refreshing and light with sweet chocolate chunks. The flavors balance each other out so it's not too rich or too tart.

Buttered Pecan

Buttered pecan is an old Southern favorite for good reason! It's the perfect decadent treat. The smooth ice cream is the perfect contrast to the crunchy pecan pieces. This is a crowd favorite because it has a little saltiness to cut through the sweetness.

Strawberry

This fruit-based flavor is a classic ice cream that goes great on its own or in a banana split. It's sweet and creamy with chunks of strawberries. This ice cream is perfect for any treat!
